# Weekly Review Checklist

> Your 30-minute weekly procedure. Each partner does this independently every Sunday.

---

## Checklist

### 1. Read Your Telegram Digest (5 min)

- [ ] Open Telegram → find your weekly portfolio summary
- [ ] Note any RED health scores (below 40)
- [ ] Note total revenue for the week
- [ ] Note any error alerts from the week

### 2. Review Shared Opportunities (5 min)

- [ ] Read the weekly opportunity digest (top 5 from [[Daily Researcher]])
- [ ] For each: mark as "pursue" / "watch" / "skip"
- [ ] If "pursue" — add a note to your [[Jeff/Decision Log|Decision Log]] or [[Patrick/Decision Log|Decision Log]]

### 3. Update Your Vault (10 min)

- [ ] Update your **Portfolio.md** strategy status table
- [ ] Update your **Revenue Tracker.md** with this week's numbers
- [ ] Log any decisions in your **Decision Log.md**
- [ ] `git add -A && git commit -m "Weekly review [date]" && git push`

### 4. Share Learnings (5 min)

- [ ] Did anything work surprisingly well? Update the strategy playbook in `Strategies/`
- [ ] Did anything fail? Note it in the playbook so the other partner can avoid it
- [ ] Any circuit breakers triggered? Document what happened

### 5. Quick Health Check (5 min)

- [ ] Is n8n running? (Check Telegram heartbeat received today)
- [ ] Any of your workflows fail this week? (Check Telegram error alerts)
- [ ] Are trading bots within risk limits? (Check drawdown in your Revenue Tracker)
- [ ] Are API costs within budget? (Check your Revenue Tracker costs)

---

## Monthly Additions (First Sunday of Month)

- [ ] Update monthly summary in your Revenue Tracker
- [ ] Calculate your portfolio ROI
- [ ] Review your API/infrastructure costs
- [ ] Consider: Should any of your strategies be killed? (Score <20 for 2+ weeks)
- [ ] Consider: Should you add a new strategy from the playbooks?
- [ ] Check in with your partner — share notes on what's working
- [ ] Update [[Dashboard]] Recent Activity section

---

## Quarterly Review (Month 3 and Month 6)

- [ ] Full portfolio valuation (revenue + asset values)
- [ ] Compare your actual vs projected in each strategy playbook
- [ ] Review all your Decision Log entries — were decisions right?
- [ ] Is the shared Daily Researcher finding useful opportunities?
- [ ] Should you reinvest revenue into more capital?
- [ ] Partner sync — compare strategies, share insights
